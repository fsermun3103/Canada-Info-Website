EXCLUSIVE EXPEDITION

3 DAYS

Northern Lights Circuit

YUKON TERRITORY

RECOMMENDED SEASON

Sept - March

Venture into the silent, frozen wilderness of the Yukon. This circuit is designed for aurora hunting, dog sledding, and celestial photography.

DAY 1

ARRIVAL IN YELLOWKNIFE

Settle into town, explore its rugged charm, and head out after dark to your first aurora hunt. Warm fires, hot drinks, and open skies frame your introduction to the northern night.

DAY 2

INTO THE WILDERNESS

Venture beyond city lights to remote lodges or teepees deep in the boreal forest. As darkness deepens, ribbons of green and violet may ripple overhead in complete silence.

DAY 3

ARCTIC CULTURE & FAREWELL GLOW

One last chase beneath wide-open skies—your final chance to witness the aurora's slow, hypnotic dance before departure.
